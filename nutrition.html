Acceptable Macronutrient Distribution Ranges
Acceptable Macronutrient Distribution Ranges (AMDRs) for carbohydrate, fat, and protein were developed and incorporated into the Dietary Reference Intakes (DRI) framework to address the growing body of evidence on macronutrient intakes and the risk of chronic diseases and prevents nutrient deficiencies.
